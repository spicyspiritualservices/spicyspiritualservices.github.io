Hi, I’m Ruthie.

I haven’t been doing this my whole life, at least not in the way I am now. For a long time, I moved through the world the way I thought I was supposed to. I followed the expectations, the roles, the “be fine, be strong, keep going” version of life that so many of us inherit.

Then, about four years ago, something shifted. It wasn’t loud. It was quiet, like a part of me I had ignored finally asking to be heard.

I started tuning into myself. Into my own happiness. Into the way my body responded to truth. Into the presence of Spirit that had been with me long before I ever acknowledged it.

Once I began listening, I couldn’t turn away from it. And I didn’t want to.

◆ My Ability
