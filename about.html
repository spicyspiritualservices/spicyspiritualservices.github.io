Hi, I’m Ruthie — intuitive educator, circle facilitator, and someone who learned intuition the long, human way.

I didn’t grow up calling myself intuitive. I learned it slowly, through paying attention, through getting it wrong, through healing, through coming back to myself again and again. Over time, I realized intuition isn’t a gift you’re “born with.” It’s a relationship you build — one that grows through awareness, regulation, boundaries, and honesty.

Today, I teach the things I had to learn the hard way: emotional literacy, discernment, spiritual foundations, and how to trust yourself without abandoning your humanity. My work is grounded, experiential, and rooted in presence rather than performance.

I lead circles, workshops, and mentorship containers for intuitives, sensitives, and spiritual learners who want to deepen their connection to themselves in a way that’s real and sustainable. I don’t teach from a pedestal. I teach from lived experience — from the moments I didn’t trust myself, from the practices that helped me return to my body, and from the community that continues to grow around this work.

Whether I’m mentoring one‑on‑one, hosting a circle, or teaching a workshop, my intention is the same: to create spaces where people feel safe to explore who they are, what they sense, and what they’re becoming.

This work is my practice. This community is my heart. And I’m honored to walk with the people who find their way here.
